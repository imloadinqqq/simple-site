New York Knicks Lose Game 5 at Home


Alex Diaz / April 30, 2025

Last night, the New York Knicks lost to the Detroit Pistons 106-103.
Both teams came out aggressive, but also inefficient with both teams shooting 40% in the first half.
The game ended with a clutch shot by OG Anunoby to cut the lead to 1, but it was too late as they had to play the foul game against the Pistons.
Despite this, the Knicks still lead the series 3-2 and will go back to Detroit for Game 6 on Thursday.

Good luck to my Knicks!!!
